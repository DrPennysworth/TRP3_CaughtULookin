<span style="color: #ced4d9;">The addon lets you has two different windows you can move, resize and toggle using a minimap Icon as well as slash commands. The Traget Window shows a list of players currently and recently targeting you. The History Window shows a history of all the people that have targeted you in the past, when they started targeting and when they stopped. Both lists let you left click the name to open their TRP3 profile or right click to open a box that lets you copy their real character username… inscase they have odd pesky characters you might not want to find the alt codes to type out.</span>

<span style="color: #e03e2d;"><strong><em>This addon requires Total RP 3 to work.</em></strong>&nbsp;</span>

Use /culook or /culookin to display all commands you can use.

Features: 

*   Shows a list of all current and recent players rageting you.
*   Shows a list of all players that have targeted you in the past. (This is saved data that presists even after closing the game.)
*   Minimap Icon to toggle either info window.
